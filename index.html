About Me

My Origin!

I was born as the son of Laufey, king of the Frost Giants of Jotunheim. Abandoned and left to die, I was adopted by Odin and raised as an Asgardian prince. I grew up alongside Thor, always in his shadow—until I chose to cast my own.

Why people like me!

I am clever, charming, and complex. People are drawn to my wit, unpredictability, and vulnerability. I'm not just a villain—I represent change, rebellion, and the possibility of redemption. Plus, Tom Hiddleston’s performance doesn’t hurt.

Am I Good or Evil?

I’ve been both, and everything in between. I’ve fought the Avengers, betrayed Thor, ruled Asgard, and helped save the timeline. I don’t fit in a box. I’m Loki. I make my own path.
